- **ML-DSA (FIPS 204)**: NIST-standardized digital signature algorithm for PQC (replaces RSA)
- **LTPA (Lightweight Third Party Authentication)**: IBM WebSphere token-based authentication mechanism

- **Crypto Modes**: Classical (RSA only), PQC (ML-DSA only), Hybrid (both)
- **IBM Java Providers**: OpenJCEPlus, IBMJCEPlus, IBMJCECCA for PQC algorithm support
- **AES-GCM**: Authenticated encryption upgrade from AES-CBC
- **Token Versioning**: LTPA v2.0 (classical) vs v3.0 (PQC)
